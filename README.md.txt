# A/B Testing Conversion Analysis

## Overview
This project analyzes the effectiveness of two marketing strategies using A/B testing methodology.

The goal was to determine whether the Ad campaign performed significantly better than the PSA campaign based on user conversion rates.

---

## Business Problem
A company launched two marketing approaches:
- Ad Campaign
- PSA Campaign

The objective was to identify which campaign generated higher user conversions.

---

## Statistical Method
Since conversion is a binary outcome (converted/not converted), a Proportion Z-Test was used instead of a T-Test.

### Hypotheses
- H₀: Conversion rates are equal
- H₁: Ad campaign performs better

---

## Results
- Ad campaign achieved a significantly higher conversion rate
- P-value < 0.05
- Null hypothesis rejected

This indicates that the Ad campaign performs better than the PSA campaign.
